Phagwah in Queens

In spring, the Phagwah parade in Queens feels so refreshing to me and reminds me that I come from a rich culture, even if I don't celebrate that culture every single day. Powder colors are everywhere in the air, there's music fills the street, and everyone is happy and a part of the moment.

The parade reminds me that culture is living. It travels from Guyana to New York, from my parents to me.

Hey, you can always go back to where the story starts, or revisit the saltfish and bake stop on Liberty Ave.
